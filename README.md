### About Me

I'm a builder from Mumbai, now based in San Francisco. I studied Data Science & Business at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, and I spend my time turning ideas into products that solve real problems.

Right now I'm all-in on **[TasksMind](https://tasksmind.com)** — an AI engineer that sits on your on-call rotation. When an incident fires, TasksMind investigates the alert, reads the code, writes a fix, and opens a PR for your team to review. No more 3 AM debugging sessions.

---

### What I'm Building

**TasksMind** — Your AI Engineer for On-Call

- Ingests alerts from PagerDuty, Datadog, Sentry, and more
- Autonomously investigates root cause across your codebase
- Generates fixes and opens pull requests with full context
- Integrates with Slack for approvals and human-in-the-loop review
- Risk-classifies changes so your team stays in control

> Ship fixes faster. Sleep through the night.
